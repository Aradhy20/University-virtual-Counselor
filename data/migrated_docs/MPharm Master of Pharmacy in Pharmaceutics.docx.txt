📘 Course Overview – MPharm in Pharmaceutics

Q1: What is MPharm in Pharmaceutics?
 Q2: MPharm Pharmaceutics kya hota hai?
 Q3: What will I study in this programme?
 Q4: Is programme mein kya sikhaya jata hai?

A:
 MPharm in Pharmaceutics is a two-year postgraduate programme that focuses on drug formulation, advanced drug delivery systems, pharmaceutical manufacturing, and regulatory aspects. It prepares students for R&D, production, and formulation development roles.
