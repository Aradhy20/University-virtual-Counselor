📘 Course Overview – MD Paediatrics

Q1: What is MD Paediatrics?
 Q2: MD Paediatrics kya hai?
 Q3: What does the course cover?
 Q4: Course me kya-kya sikhaya jata hai?
 Q5: Is it a clinical programme?
 Q6: Kya yeh clinical programme hai?

A:
 MD Paediatrics is a 3-year clinical postgraduate programme focused on the healthcare of infants, children, and adolescents. It trains students in diagnosis, treatment, and child health management from birth to 18 years.
 For more information, visit: https://www.tmu.ac.in/programme/md-paediatrics
