Course Overview – MSc in Radiological Imaging Techniques

Q1: What is MSc in Radiological Imaging Techniques all about?
 Q2: MSc Radiology me kya padhaya jata hai?
 Q3: Kya is course me advanced imaging technologies ka use sikhaya jata hai?

A:
 The MSc in Radiological Imaging Techniques is a postgraduate programme focused on developing expertise in diagnostic imaging, including CT, MRI, ultrasound, and digital radiography. It equips students with theoretical knowledge and clinical skills essential for accurate imaging and patient care in medical diagnostics.

🧑‍🏫 Teaching Methods – MSc in Radiological Imaging Techniques

Q1: Kya teaching me practical training hoti hai?
 Q2: What kind of learning approach is used in MSc Radiology programme?

A:
 The teaching approach includes live imaging sessions, lab-based simulations, expert lectures, digital diagnostics, clinical rotations, and use of state-of-the-art imaging equipment like CT, MRI, and Doppler systems.
