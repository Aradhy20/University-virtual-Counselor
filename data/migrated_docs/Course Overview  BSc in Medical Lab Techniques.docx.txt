Course Overview – BSc in Medical Lab Techniques

Q1: BSc Medical Lab Techniques course kya hai?
 Q2: What does the BSc in Medical Lab Techniques programme include?

A:
 BSc in Medical Lab Techniques trains students to become skilled laboratory professionals who assist in diagnosis and treatment. It includes clinical chemistry, pathology, hematology, and microbiology to equip students with lab-based diagnostic expertise.
