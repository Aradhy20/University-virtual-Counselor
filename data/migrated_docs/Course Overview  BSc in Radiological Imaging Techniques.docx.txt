Course Overview – BSc in Radiological Imaging Techniques

Q1: BSc Radiological Imaging Techniques kya course hai?
 Q2: What do students learn in BSc Radiological Imaging Techniques?

A:
 BSc in Radiological Imaging Techniques trains students to use diagnostic imaging equipment like X-ray, CT, MRI, and ultrasound. It prepares them to assist radiologists in patient diagnosis through modern imaging technologies.
