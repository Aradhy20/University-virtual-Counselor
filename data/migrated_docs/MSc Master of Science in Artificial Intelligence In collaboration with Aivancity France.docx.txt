Tell me about MSc (Master of Science) in Artificial Intelligence (In collaboration with Aivancity, France)

M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a two-year postgraduate program that focuses on advanced AI concepts, machine learning, deep learning, and data science. The course is designed to equip students with theoretical knowledge and practical skills in AI technologies, preparing them for research, innovation, and industry applications.
