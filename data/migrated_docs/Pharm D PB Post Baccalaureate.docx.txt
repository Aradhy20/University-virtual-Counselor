Pharm D PB (Post Baccalaureate)

Tell me about Pharm D PB (Post Baccalaureate)

Pharm D (Post Baccalaureate) is a three-year advanced programme designed for those who have completed B Pharm

. This programme allows graduates of the B.Pharm to advance their education and clinical expertise by focusing on clinical pharmacy, patient care, and pharmaceutical research.  The curriculum includes core subjects like Advanced Pharmacology and Clinical Research

, along with practical training through internships. Pharm D (Post Baccalaureate) programme Eligibility requires a B.Pharm degree from the candidates, and after completion of the degree, graduates can pursue careers in clinical pharmacy, research, academia, and various roles within the pharmaceutical industry..
