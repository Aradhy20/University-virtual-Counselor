Course Overview – MD General Medicine

Q1: What is MD General Medicine?
 Q2: MD General Medicine kya hai?
 Q3: What does this course teach?
 Q4: Course me kya sikhate hain?
 Q5: Is it a clinical programme?
 Q6: Kya yeh clinical field hai?

A:
 MD General Medicine is a 3-year postgraduate clinical programme focusing on diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of adult diseases. It trains students in internal medicine through theoretical learning and practical exposure.
 For details, visit: https://www.tmu.ac.in/programme/md-general-medicine
