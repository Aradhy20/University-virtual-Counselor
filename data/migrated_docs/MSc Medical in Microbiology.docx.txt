Tell me about MSc Medical in Microbiology

MSc Medical in Microbiology is a two-year postgraduate programme spread across four semesters

. MSc Medical in Microbiology programme focuses on the study of microorganisms and their role in human health, covering subjects such as General Microbiology, Immunology, Clinical Microbiology, Virology, and Research Methodology. To be eligible for MSc Medical in Microbiology programme

, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in a relevant field with a minimum required percentage according to the programme.MSc Medical in Microbiology programme’s graduates can pursue their careers in clinical microbiology, research, quality control, academia, and public health.
