MS (Master of Science) in ENT/Oto-Rhinlaryngology

Tell me about MS (Master of Science) in ENT/Oto-Rhinlaryngology

MS (Master of Science) ENT is a three-year post-graduate programme aimed at providing in-depth knowledge of diseases related to the ear, nose, throat and head and neck. The programme trains students to plan and advise measures for the promotive, precautionary, restorative and rehabilitative aspects of health and ailments in the speciality of ENT.
