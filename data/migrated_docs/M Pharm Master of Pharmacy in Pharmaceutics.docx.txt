M Pharm (Master of Pharmacy) in Pharmaceutics

Tell me about M Pharm (Master of Pharmacy) in Pharmaceutics

M Pharm (Master of Pharmacy) Pharmaceutics is a two-year postgraduate programme, divided into four semesters. M Pharm Pharmaceutics focuses on the science of drug formulation, development, and manufacturing

. The programme covers a range of subjects or topics such as advanced drug delivery systems, regulatory affairs, and industrial pharmacy. To be eligible for the M Pharm (Master of Pharmacy) Pharmaceutics programme, candidates must hold a Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm) degree from a recognised institution or university

, with a minimum aggregate score. M Pharm (Master of Pharmacy) Pharmaceutics programme’s graduates have various career opportunities in the pharmaceutical industry, research and development (R&D), academia, or regulatory bodies, for multiple roles, which makes this an ideal choice for those looking to advance in the pharmaceutical field.
