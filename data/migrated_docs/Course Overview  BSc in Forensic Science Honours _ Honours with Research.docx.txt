Course Overview – BSc in Forensic Science (Honours / Honours with Research)

Q1: BSc Forensic Science course kya hota hai?
 Q2: What is the Forensic Science (Honours) programme about?

A:
 The BSc in Forensic Science (Honours / Honours with Research) is a specialized programme that prepares students for crime investigation, forensic analysis, and lab-based evidence assessment. It combines scientific methods with legal applications, covering toxicology, DNA analysis, and crime scene investigation.
