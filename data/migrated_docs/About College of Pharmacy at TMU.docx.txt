A:
 Teerthanker Mahaveer University’s College of Pharmacy, established in 2008 and approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), offers Diploma, BPharm, MPharm, Pharm D, and PhD programmes. It blends academic research with real‑world pharmaceutical problem solving and clinical training.

🎯 Eligibility and Selection – College of Pharmacy

Q1: What is the eligibility for BPharm and MPharm at TMU?
 Q2: Pharmacy ke liye admission criteria kya hai?
 Q3: Kya 12th Science wale Pharmacy apply kar sakte hain?
 Q4: MPharm admission ke liye kya qualification chahiye?
 Q5: Pharm D ke liye kya eligibility hai?
 Q6: TMU mein pharmacy admission merit based hai ya entrance based?

A:
 Eligibility differs by course. For BPharm, 10+2 with PCM/B is required. For MPharm, a BPharm degree is needed. Pharm D requires 10+2 with Science or DPharm. Selections are based on merit and counselling.

🕒 Duration – College of Pharmacy
