Q: What is Cloud Computing?

A: Cloud computing is a technology that enables on-demand access to a shared pool of configurable computing resources, such as servers, storage, databases, networking, software, and applications, over the internet. Instead of owning and maintaining physical infrastructure, users can rent computing resources from cloud service providers on a pay-as-you-go basis.
