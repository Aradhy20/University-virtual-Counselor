TMU Corporate Resource Centre (Placement Cell)

Q1: What is the role of the Corporate Resource Centre at TMU?
 Q2: CRC ka kya kaam hota hai TMU me?
 Q3: How does the placement cell help students in getting jobs?
 Q4: TMU ke placement cell ke under kaun kaun si activities hoti hain?
 Q5: Kya CRC personality development aur interview training bhi karwata hai?
 Q6: What kind of companies come for placement at TMU?

Q7: TMU me kitni placement offers students ko mil chuki hain?

A:

The Corporate Resource Centre (CRC) at TMU plays a crucial role in preparing students for placements and professional success. With over 2500+ placement offers, the CRC has helped students land jobs with packages as high as ₹60 LPA (national), ₹32 LPA (international), ₹20 LPA (CCSIT), and ₹15 LPA (MBA)

. The centre conducts mock interviews, aptitude tests, body language coaching, communication training, and soft skills workshops to ensure students are career-ready.

The CRC fosters strong industry-academia partnerships and has attracted over 400 companies across various sectors, including engineering, management, and healthcare. It facilitates internships, industry visits, and job opportunities at both the university and college levels.

For more info, visit:https://www.tmu.ac.in/tmu/corporate-resource-centre
 Note: Placement opportunities depend on eligibility, performance, and recruitment policies of individual companies.
 To connect with the CRC team, please reach out through your college placement coordinator or visit the CRC office on campus.
