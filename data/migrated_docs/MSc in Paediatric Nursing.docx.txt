MSc in Paediatric Nursing

Tell me about MSc in Paediatric Nursing

MSc in Paediatric Nursing is a two-year postgraduate programme, divided into four semesters. MSc in Paediatric Nursing programme is designed to equip nurses with advanced skills and specialised knowledge in paediatric care

. The MSc in Paediatric Nursing programme curriculum includes subjects such as Advanced Nursing Practice, Child Health Nursing, Paediatric Intensive Care, and Nursing Research. To be eligible for MSc in Paediatric Nursing programme

, candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Nursing (BSc Nursing) and eligibility may also require some work experience in a clinical setting. MSc in Paediatric Nursing programme’s graduates can pursue their careers in hospitals, neonatal care units, schools, and community health centres, for various roles or continue with further studies and research.
