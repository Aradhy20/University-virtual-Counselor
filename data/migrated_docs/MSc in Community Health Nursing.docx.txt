MSc in Community Health Nursing

Tell me about MSc in Community Health Nursing

MSc in Community Health Nursing is a two-year postgraduate programme spread across four semesters

. The MSc in Community Health Nursing programme focuses on enhancing the health and well-being of communities by addressing public health challenges through education, research, and practical interventions. To be eligible for the MSc in Community Health Nursing programme

, candidates must hold a BSc Nursing degree with at least 55% marks and possess one year of relevant work experience. MSc in Community Health Nursing programme’s graduates are well-equipped for careers as Community Health Nurses, Public Health Educators, Health Policy Analysts, and more, contributing to improved community health outcomes.
