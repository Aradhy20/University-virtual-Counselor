MSc Medical in Pharmacology

Tell me about MSc Medical in Pharmacology

MSc Medical in Pharmacology is a two-year postgraduate programme spanning four semesters. The MSc Medical in Pharmacology programme deals with the study of drugs and their effects, and their therapeutic uses. The programme curriculum includes subjects like pharmacokinetics, toxicology, drug development, and clinical pharmacology. To be eligible for M.Sc

. Medical in Pharmacology programme, Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy, Medicine, life sciences or a related field, with a minimum aggregate score as required. M.Sc

. Medical in Pharmacology programme Graduates can start their professional career in the field of Pharmaceuticals, Clinical Research, Regulatory Affairs for various job roles, or pursue other postgraduate studies such as a Ph.D. in Pharmacology.
