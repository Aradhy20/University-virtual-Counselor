What is the eligibility for BPEd?

Bachelor degree in Physical Education (BPE/ B.Sc./B.A. in Physical Education) or graduation in any stream with Physical Education as an elective subject with minimum 50% marks in aggregate. (45% for participants to national/ inter university sports competitions recognized by AIU or IOA and for in-service trained physical education teachers/ coaches with 3 years teaching experience)
