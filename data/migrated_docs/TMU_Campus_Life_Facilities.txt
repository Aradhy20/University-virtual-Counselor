TMU Campus Life and Facilities
================================

Answer:

TMU ka campus 140-acre ka green campus hai jo Delhi Road (NH-24), Moradabad mein located hai.

CAMPUS FACILITIES:
1. Central Library: 1,00,000+ books, e-journals, digital resources, reading rooms, WiFi
2. Wi-Fi Campus: Pure campus mein free WiFi available hai
3. Sports Complex: Cricket ground, Football field, Basketball court, Badminton, Swimming Pool, Indoor Gym, Athletics Track
4. Auditorium: 2000+ seating capacity ka modern auditorium
5. Hospital: TMU Medical College Hospital — 500+ beds, fully equipped, community service
6. Cafeteria & Food Courts: Multiple eating options — North Indian, South Indian, Fast Food
7. Hostels: Separate boys aur girls hostels, well-furnished rooms with cooler/AC options
8. 24x7 Security: CCTV cameras, security guards, gated campus — girls hostel pe strict monitoring
9. ATM & Post Office: Campus ke andar hi banking aur postal services
10. Shopping Complex: Daily essentials, stationery, photocopying — sab campus mein milta hai
11. Transport: University buses available hain Moradabad city ke different routes pe
12. Medical Facility: 24x7 emergency medical assistance within campus
13. Labs & Workshops: State-of-the-art laboratories for all engineering, medical, and science programs

CULTURAL & EVENTS:
- Annual Cultural Fest: "Mahaveer Mahotsav" — 3-day mega cultural event
- Technical Fest: Hackathons, coding competitions, robotics
- Sports Meet: Inter-college aur inter-university championships
- Annual Convocation
- Guest Lectures: Industry leaders aur academic experts by invite
- NSS & NCC: Social service aur national cadet corps activities

RAGGING:
- TMU ek 100% Ragging-Free Campus hai
- Anti-Ragging Committee active rehti hai
- "Didi-Bhaiya" culture hai — senior students bahut supportive hain
- Strict action against any ragging complaints

LOCATION & CONNECTIVITY:
- Moradabad Railway Station se 10-15 km
- Delhi se NH-24 pe 3-3.5 ghante drive
- Nearest Airport: Delhi IGI Airport (approx 170 km)
- Regular buses from Delhi, Lucknow, Bareilly available

For more info, visit: https://www.tmu.ac.in/campus-life
