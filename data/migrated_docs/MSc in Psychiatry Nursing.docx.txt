MSc in Psychiatry Nursing

Tell me about MSc in Psychiatry Nursing

MSc in Psychiatry Nursing is a postgraduate programme with a duration period of two years divided into four semesters. This programme provides students with sophisticated knowledge and practical skills concerning mental health care

. The curriculum includes topics such as Advanced Psychiatric Nursing, Psychopharmacology, and Psychosocial Rehabilitation etc. To be eligible for the MSc (N) in Psychiatry Nursing programme, candidates shall be those who have passed a bachelor’s degree in Nursing (such as B.Sc

. Nursing) with an aggregate score as required.MSc in Psychiatry Nursing programme graduates are likely to find themselves being employed in psychiatric nursing, clinical nurse specialisation, and mental health counselling careers in hospitals, among other health facilities.
