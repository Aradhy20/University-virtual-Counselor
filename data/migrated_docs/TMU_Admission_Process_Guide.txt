TMU Admission Process — Complete Guide
========================================

Q1: TMU mein admission kaise hota hai? What is the admission process?
Q2: Online apply kaise karein? How to apply online at TMU?
Q3: Admission ke liye kya documents chahiye?
Q4: Kya entrance exam dena zaroori hai?
Q5: Merit-based admission kaise milta hai?
Q6: Admission form ki fee kitni hai?
Q7: Last date kab hai admission ki?

Answer:

TMU mein admission lene ke liye aap online ya offline dono tarike se apply kar sakte hain:

STEP 1: Visit tmu.ac.in/apply aur online application form bharein.
STEP 2: Required documents upload karein:
  - 10th Marksheet
  - 12th Marksheet (ya equivalent)
  - Aadhaar Card
  - Passport size photo
  - Category certificate (agar applicable ho)
  - Migration/Transfer certificate
STEP 3: Application fee pay karein (online payment available hai).
STEP 4: Document verification hogi.
STEP 5: Fee payment karein aur seat confirm karein.

ADMISSION ROUTES:
- B.Tech: JEE Main score ya TMU Entrance Test (TMUEE) ya 12th merit.
- MBBS/BDS: NEET score mandatory hai.
- MBA: CAT/MAT/CMAT score ya TMU entrance test.
- Law (BA LLB/LLB): CLAT score ya TMU entrance.
- B.Sc Nursing: NEET score required.
- Other UG Programs: 12th marks merit-based ya CUET score.
- PG Programs: Graduation marks merit-based.

IMPORTANT:
- Admissions are generally open from January to August (subject to seat availability).
- Some courses have rolling admissions — pehle aao pehle paao.
