Tell me about MSc Medical in Biochemistry

MSc Medical in Biochemistry is a two-year postgraduate programme, divided into four semesters. The MSc Medical in Biochemistry programme provides an in-depth study of biochemical processes in medical science. The programme curriculum includes topics such as molecular biology, clinical biochemistry, enzymology, and metabolism. To be eligible for M.Sc

. Medical in Biochemistry programme, Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in biochemistry or a related field with a minimum aggregate score as required. MSc Medical in Biochemistry programme graduates can pursue careers as clinical biochemists, research scientists, laboratory managers, and in different roles in the pharmaceutical industry, academia, and diagnostic labs.
