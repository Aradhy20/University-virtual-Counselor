Answer:

TMU ka College of Nursing INC (Indian Nursing Council) aur UP State Nursing Council approved hai. Clinical training TMU Medical College Hospital (500+ beds) mein hoti hai jo campus ke andar hi hai.

PROGRAMS OFFERED:

1. B.Sc Nursing — 4-year Program
   - Eligibility: 12th pass (PCB — Physics, Chemistry, Biology) with minimum 45% marks
   - Age: 17 years minimum at time of admission
   - Admission: NEET score required
   - Duration: 4 years (8 semesters) + 6 months internship
   - Clinical Training: TMU Hospital (500+ beds) — Hands-on patient care experience

2. Post Basic B.Sc Nursing — 2-year Program
   - Eligibility: GNM (General Nursing & Midwifery) diploma + Registered Nurse
   - Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)
   - Admission: Merit-based

3. M.Sc Nursing — 2-year PG Program
   - Eligibility: B.Sc Nursing with minimum 55% marks + 1 year clinical experience
   - Duration: 2 years
   - Specializations:
     a) Medical-Surgical Nursing
     b) Obstetrics & Gynaecology Nursing
     c) Paediatric Nursing
     d) Psychiatry/Mental Health Nursing
     e) Community Health Nursing

APPROVAL: Indian Nursing Council (INC) approved, UP State Nursing Council approved

KEY FEATURES:
- Clinical Training at TMU Hospital: 500+ bed hospital on campus — real patient exposure
- Simulation Lab: Advanced nursing simulation laboratory for practical training
- Community Health Programs: Field visits to rural health centres
- Library: Nursing journals, reference books, digital resources
- Hostel: Dedicated nursing hostel with warden facility (generally compulsory for BSc Nursing)
- International Standards: Curriculum aligned with global nursing practices

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES AFTER NURSING:
- Staff Nurse (Government/Private hospitals)
- ICU/OT/Emergency Nurse Specialist
- Community Health Nurse
- Nursing Superintendent
- Nursing Educator/Professor
- Public Health Officer
- Military Nursing Service (MNS)
- Opportunities abroad (UK, USA, Australia, Gulf countries — after additional registration)
