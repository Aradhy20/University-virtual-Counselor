Teerthanker Mahaveer University (TMU) ek leading private university hai jo Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, India mein located hai.

KEY FACTS:
- Established: 2008 (Uttar Pradesh State Legislature Act)
- Campus: 140-acre green campus, Delhi Road (NH-24), Moradabad
- Accreditation: NAAC 'A' Grade
- Approvals: UGC, AICTE, BCI, PCI, NCTE, NMC (formerly MCI), DCI, INC approved
- Total Colleges: 14+ constituent colleges
- Programs: 200+ courses across UG, PG, Diploma, and Doctoral levels
- Students: 15,000+ students from across India and abroad
