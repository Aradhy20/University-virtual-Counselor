About MDS in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics

1. Course Overview – MDS in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics

Q1: What is MDS in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics?
 Q2: MDS Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics kya hota hai?
 Q3: Is programme mein kya sikhaaya jaata hai?
 Q4: Yeh course kis field mein specialize karta hai?
 Q5: Kya yeh root canal aur dental restoration cover karta hai?
 Q6: MDS in Endodontics ka focus kya hai?

A:
 MDS in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics is a 3-year postgraduate programme focused on diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases and injuries of the tooth pulp and surrounding tissues. It includes procedures like root canal therapy and restoration.
