MSc Medical in Physiology

Tell me about MSc Medical in Physiology

MSc Medical in Physiology is a two-year postgraduate programme, spanning into 4 semesters. The MSc Medical in Physiology programme focuses on understanding the functions of the human body at both cellular and systemic levels

. The programme curriculum covers core subjects such as cardiovascular, nervous, and endocrine systems. To be eligible for the MSc Medical in Physiology programme, candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in Life Sciences, Medicine, or a related field with minimum aggregate marks as required. MSc Medical in Physiology programme graduates can pursue their careers in hospitals, research labs

, research labs, or academia, for various roles

. Graduates also have opportunities for further studies, like a PhD in Physiology.
