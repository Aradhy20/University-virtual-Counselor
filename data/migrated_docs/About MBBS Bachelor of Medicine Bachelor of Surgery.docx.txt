Ans:
Eligibility Criteria
 Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB), achieving a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate and a pass mark in English.

Criteria of Merit
 Admission to the MBBS programme is strictly based on NEET‑UG merit.

Have questions about the eligibility or admission process? Contact our Admissions Team at 9568518000 or visit the portal: https://admissions.tmu.ac.in/
