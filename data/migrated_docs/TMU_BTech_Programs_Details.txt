Answer:

TMU ke B.Tech programs AICTE approved hain aur strong industry connections ke saath quality engineering education provide karte hain.

B.TECH BRANCHES AVAILABLE:

Under College of Computing Sciences & IT (CCSIT):
1. B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) — AICTE Approved
2. B.Tech CSE — Data Science
3. B.Tech CSE — Cyber Security
4. B.Tech CSE — Cloud Technology & Information Security
5. B.Tech CSE — Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Under College of Engineering (COE):
6. B.Tech Mechanical Engineering
7. B.Tech Civil Engineering
8. B.Tech Electrical Engineering
9. B.Tech Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

ELIGIBILITY:
- 12th pass with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)
- Minimum 45% marks in 12th
- JEE Main score accepted (preferred but not mandatory for all branches)
- CUET score also accepted
- Direct admission available on 12th merit basis

ADMISSION ROUTES:
1. JEE Main Score Based: Apply with JEE rank
2. 12th Merit Based: Direct admission on PCM percentage
3. CUET Score: Accepted for admission
4. TMU Entrance Test: TMUEE

DURATION: 4 years (8 semesters)

KEY FEATURES:
- AICTE Approved: All B.Tech programs are AICTE approved
- Modern Labs: IoT Lab, AI/ML Lab, Robotics Lab, Network Security Lab
- Industry Partnerships: Tie-ups with Microsoft, AWS, Google, IBM
- Internships: Mandatory summer internships in 6th semester
- Projects: Final year projects with industry mentors
- Coding Culture: Regular hackathons, coding contests, tech fests
- StartUp Incubation: TMU supports student startups

PLACEMENT HIGHLIGHTS:
- Highest Package: ₹60 LPA (National)
- Average Package: ₹5-7 LPA
- Top Recruiters: TCS, Wipro, Infosys, HCL, Cognizant, Deloitte, Amazon, Capgemini, Accenture
- 100% Placement Assistance through CRC (Corporate Resource Centre)
- Pre-placement training: Aptitude, coding, soft skills, mock interviews

NOTE ON FEES:
- For exact, current fee structure, please contact the admissions team
- Contact: 1800-270-7373 (Toll-Free) or admissions@tmu.ac.in
- Fee structure varies by branch and specialization
- Payment can be made semester-wise
- Education loan facility available through partner banks
