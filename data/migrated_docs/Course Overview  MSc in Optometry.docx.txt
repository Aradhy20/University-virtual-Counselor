Course Overview – MSc in Optometry

Q1: What is MSc in Optometry about?
 Q2: MSc Optometry mein kya padhaya jaata hai?
 Q3: Can you give a brief overview of the MSc in Optometry programme?
 Q4: MSc in Optometry course mein kis type ka training milta hai?

A:
 MSc in Optometry is a postgraduate programme that trains students in advanced visual health sciences, low vision aids, ocular diagnostics, and clinical procedures. It prepares graduates to serve as skilled optometry professionals in hospitals, clinics, and academia.

Q1: MSc in Optometry ka curriculum kya cover karta hai?
 Q2: What topics are taught in MSc Optometry?

A:
 The curriculum includes advanced contact lens fitting, low vision rehabilitation, binocular vision, ocular pathology, and optometric instrumentation. Students also undergo clinical postings.
