Answer:

TMU ka College of Fine Arts creative students ke liye ek excellent platform provide karta hai. Traditional aur modern art forms dono cover hote hain yahan.

PROGRAMS OFFERED:

1. BFA — Bachelor of Fine Arts
   - Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
   - Eligibility: 12th pass (any stream) with minimum 45% marks
   - Admission: Portfolio review + Drawing test + Merit
   - Specializations: Painting, Sculpture, Applied Arts, Visual Communication

2. MFA — Master of Fine Arts
   - Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)
   - Eligibility: BFA or equivalent degree with minimum 50% marks
   - Admission: Portfolio review + Interview

KEY SUBJECTS:
- Drawing & Sketching (Foundation)
- Painting (Oil, Watercolor, Acrylic)
- Sculpture & 3D Art
- Applied Arts & Graphic Design
- Art History (Indian & Western)
- Digital Art & Multimedia
- Photography
- Printmaking & Illustration
- Typography & Layout Design

KEY FEATURES:
- Dedicated Art Studios: Spacious, well-lit studios for painting and sculpture
- Exhibition Galleries: Regular student exhibitions and art shows
- Art Workshops: Guest artists and professionals conduct workshops
- Field Visits: Art gallery visits, heritage site tours, outdoor sketching sessions
- Annual Art Exhibition: Student work displayed for public and industry
- Modern Tools: Digital art tablets, design software (Adobe Suite)
- Interdisciplinary Learning: Collaboration with Media, Architecture, Design teams

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES AFTER FINE ARTS:
- Professional Artist/Painter/Sculptor
- Graphic Designer
- UI/UX Designer
- Art Director (Advertising/Film/Media)
- Interior Designer (with additional course)
- Animation & VFX Artist
- Art Teacher/Professor
- Museum Curator
- Freelance Illustrator
- Textile/Fashion Designer
