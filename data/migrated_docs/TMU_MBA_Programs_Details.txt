Answer:

TMU ka MBA program AICTE approved hai aur dual specialization option ke saath industry-ready managers taioyaar karta hai.

PROGRAMS OFFERED:

1. MBA (Dual Specialization) — 2-year Program
   - Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)
   - Eligibility: Graduation (any discipline) with minimum 50% marks
   - Admission: CAT / MAT / CMAT / XAT score OR TMU Entrance Test
   - Specializations (choose any 2):
     a) Marketing Management
     b) Finance Management
     c) Human Resource Management (HRM)
     d) International Business
     e) Operations Management
     f) Information Technology (IT)
     g) Business Analytics

2. MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management — 2-year Program
   - Duration: 2 years
   - Focus: Supply chain, warehousing, transportation management
   - Industry visits to logistics companies

3. MBA Corporate with Global Exposure — 2-year Program
   - Duration: 2 years
   - Unique: International exposure module with foreign university visit
   - Focus: Global business practices aur cross-cultural management

KEY FEATURES:
- AICTE Approved: MBA programs are AICTE approved
- Dual Specialization: Students choose 2 areas for broader career scope
- Case Study Method: Harvard-style case study teaching approach
- Industry Projects: Live projects with companies
- Summer Internship: Mandatory 6-8 week summer internship after 2nd semester
- Guest Lectures: CEOs, MDs, industry leaders weekly sessions
- Entrepreneurship Cell: Support for student entrepreneurs
- Management Fest & Competitions: Regular B-school competitions

PLACEMENT HIGHLIGHTS:
- Top Recruiters: Deloitte, Ernst & Young, HDFC Bank, ICICI, Amazon, Flipkart
- Average Package: ₹5-8 LPA for MBA graduates
- 100% Placement Assistance
- CRC (Corporate Resource Centre) provides placement training:
  - Group Discussion practice
  - Personal Interview coaching
  - Resume building workshops
  - Aptitude & Reasoning training

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES AFTER MBA:
- Marketing Manager / Brand Manager

- HR Manager / Talent Acquisition Specialist
- Operations Manager
- Business Consultant
- Supply Chain Manager
- Digital Marketing Manager
- Business Analyst
