Course Overview – MSc in Forensic Science

Q1: What is the MSc in Forensic Science programme about?
 Q2: MSc Forensic Science me kya padhaya jata hai?
 Q3: Kya forensic analysis aur investigation ka practical training milta hai?

A:
 The MSc in Forensic Science programme is designed to provide advanced education in the scientific principles of crime investigation, forensic chemistry, toxicology, serology, DNA analysis, and digital forensics. It prepares students to work with law enforcement and forensic agencies, equipping them with both theoretical and hands-on forensic training.

🎓 Eligibility & Selection – MSc in Forensic Science

Q1: MSc Forensic Science ke liye eligibility kya hai?
 Q2: Who can apply for MSc in Forensic Science at TMU?

A:
 Graduates with a BSc degree in Forensic Science or relevant subjects such as Chemistry, Biochemistry, Biotechnology, Zoology, or equivalent are eligible. Selection is based on academic merit and university norms.
