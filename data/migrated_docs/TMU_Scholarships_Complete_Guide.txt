Answer:

TMU mein kayi tarah ki scholarships aur fee concessions available hain jisse students ki education affordable ho jaaye.

SCHOLARSHIP SCHEMES:

1. JAIN MINORITY SCHOLARSHIP
   - Eligibility: Students belonging to Jain community
   - Benefit: 50% tuition fee waiver
   - Documents: Jain community certificate from recognized authority
   - Application: At the time of admission

2. MERIT SCHOLARSHIP
   - For 12th Marks:
     * 95% aur upar: Special merit scholarship (up to 100% tuition fee waiver)
     * 90% - 94.99%: Significant fee concession
     * 85% - 89.99%: Partial fee concession
   - For competitive exam toppers (JEE, NEET top ranks): Special packages
   - Renewed annually based on academic performance at TMU

3. SPORTS QUOTA SCHOLARSHIP
   - Eligibility: National/State level sports achievements
   - Level: Fee concession based on level of achievement
   - Required: Sports certificates, medal certificates from recognized bodies
   - Sports: Cricket, Football, Basketball, Athletics, Badminton, etc.

4. GOVERNMENT SCHOLARSHIPS (Facilitating)
   - SC/ST/OBC scholarships (Government of UP/Central Government)
   - EWS (Economically Weaker Section) concessions
   - PM Vidyalaxmi scheme
   - TMU helps students apply for government scholarship portals

5. SIBLING DISCOUNT
   - Eligibility: If a sibling is already studying at TMU
   - Benefit: Fee concession on second child's admission
   - Documents: Sibling's TMU ID card and enrollment proof

6. EDUCATION LOAN ASSISTANCE
   - TMU has tie-ups with multiple banks for education loans
   - Banks: SBI, PNB, Bank of Baroda, HDFC Credila, Axis Bank
   - Loan process guidance provided by admission office
   - Documents required: Income proof, admission letter, fee structure

HOW TO APPLY:
- Declare scholarship eligibility at the time of admission
- Submit supporting documents to scholarship cell
- Merit scholarships are auto-applied based on 12th marks
- Jain scholarship requires community certificate
- Sports scholarship requires certificates of achievement
- Contact scholarship cell: scholarship@tmu.ac.in

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Scholarships apply to TUITION FEE only (hostel/mess charges separate)
- Merit scholarship renewal depends on maintaining minimum CGPA
- Scholarships cannot be combined (only one scheme applicable at a time)
- 50% Jain scholarship is the most availed scholarship at TMU
