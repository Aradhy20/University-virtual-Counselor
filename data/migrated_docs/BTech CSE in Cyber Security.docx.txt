Tell me about BTech CSE in Cyber Security

BTech in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialisation in Cyber Security is a 4-year undergraduate degree programme that combines core computer science education with advanced knowledge of cyber security principles, tools, and practices

. This programme is designed to equip students with the technical expertise to protect digital systems, networks, and data from cyber threats and attacks.The BTech CSE programme at TMU integrates core computer science subjects with specialised programmes in cybersecurity. Students gain hands-on experience through practical labs, workshops, and industry projects

. The curriculum is aligned with current industry standards, ensuring graduates are well-prepared for careers in various sectors of the IT industry.

Frequently Asked Questions about BTech CSE in Cyber Security

Q: What is BTech CSE in Cyber Security?

A: BTech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) with a specialisation in Cyber Security is a four-year undergraduate programme that combines core computer science education with in-depth knowledge of cyber security

. Students learn foundational subjects like programming, data structures, algorithms, databases, and networks, alongside specialised topics such as ethical hacking, cryptography, network security, digital forensics, and cyber laws. The programme aims to equip students with the skills to develop secure software systems and protect digital infrastructure from cyber threats.

Q: BTech CSE in Cyber Security Admission Process

A: The admission process for the BTech CSE in Cyber Security programme at TMU is designed to be simple and accessible. Interested candidates can apply through both online and offline modes. The application form is available on the official university website. To be eligible, candidates must have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects

. A minimum of 50% aggregate marks is required, and English must have been one of the subjects studied at the qualifying level.
