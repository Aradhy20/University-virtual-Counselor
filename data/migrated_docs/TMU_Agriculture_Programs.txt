Answer:

TMU ka College of Agriculture ek modern, research-oriented agriculture education provide karta hai. Campus mein dedicated agricultural farm aur laboratories hain.

PROGRAMS OFFERED:

1. B.Sc Agriculture (Hons) — 4-year Program
   - Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
   - Eligibility: 12th pass with PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) OR PCM, minimum 50% marks
   - Admission: Merit-based on 12th marks / CUET
   - Includes: Field training, rural awareness programs, industrial visits

2. M.Sc Agriculture — 2-year PG Program
   - Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)
   - Eligibility: B.Sc Agriculture with minimum 55% marks
   - Specializations:
     a) Agronomy
     b) Soil Science
     c) Plant Pathology
     d) Horticulture
     e) Entomology

KEY SUBJECTS:
- Crop Production & Management
- Soil Science & Soil Fertility
- Plant Pathology & Pest Management
- Horticulture (Fruits, Vegetables, Flowers)
- Agricultural Economics & Marketing
- Animal Husbandry & Dairy Science
- Seed Technology
- Agri-Business Management
- Farm Machinery & Power
- Organic Farming

KEY FEATURES:
- On-Campus Farm: Dedicated agricultural land for practical training
- Modern Labs: Soil testing lab, plant tissue culture lab, seed technology lab
- Field Visits: Government farms, agricultural research stations, rural areas
- READY Program: Rural Entrepreneurship Awareness Development Yojana
- Industry Exposure: Agri-tech companies, food processing units visits
- Research Projects: Final year research dissertation with faculty guidance

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES AFTER BSC AGRICULTURE:
- Agriculture Officer (Government — UPSC/State services)
- Bank Agricultural Field Officer (IBPS/SBI)
- Agronomist / Crop Specialist
- Agriculture Scientist (after M.Sc/PhD)
- Agri-Business Manager
- Food Processing & Quality Control
- Seed/Fertilizer Company Representative
- Farm Manager
- Agriculture Journalist
- Self-Employment: Farm ownership, nursery, organic farming

GOVERNMENT JOBS:
- ICAR (Indian Council of Agricultural Research) — Scientist posts
- State Agriculture Department — Agriculture Development Officer
- Banks — Agriculture Field Officer (AFO)
- FCI (Food Corporation of India)
- Krishi Vigyan Kendra — Subject Matter Specialist
