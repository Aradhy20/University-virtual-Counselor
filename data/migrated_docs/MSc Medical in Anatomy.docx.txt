MSc Medical in Anatomy

Tell me about MSc Medical in Anatomy

MSc Medical in Anatomy is a two-year postgraduate programme, divided into 4 semesters. The MSc Medical in Anatomy programme focuses on the comprehensive study of human anatomy

. The programme curriculum covers key topics such as Gross Anatomy, Embryology, Histology, Neuroanatomy, and Applied Anatomy. To be eligible for the MSc Medical Anatomy programme, candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Medical Science or a related field with a minimum aggregate score as required. Graduates of the MSc Medical in Anatomy programme can pursue careers in academia

, research institutes, healthcare organisations, and hospitals for various related roles.
