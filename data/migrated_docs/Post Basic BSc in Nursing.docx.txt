Post Basic BSc in Nursing

Tell me about Post Basic BSc in Nursing

Post Basic BSc Nursing is a three-year undergraduate programme which is designed for registered nurses who wish to advance their skills and knowledge in nursing practice. The programme is structured into four semesters, covering an extensive syllabus that includes subjects such as Nursing Foundation, Community Health Nursing, Medical-Surgical Nursing, Mental Health Nursing, and more

. To be eligible for the Post Basic BSc Nursing programme, Applicants must have completed a General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) programme with the minimum aggregate score as required. Post Basic BSc Nursing programme graduates can look forward to diverse career opportunities in healthcare settings, including roles in hospitals, community health services, and educational institutions.
