MSc Plant Pathology

Tell me about MSc Plant Pathology

MSc in Plant Pathology is a two-year postgraduate programme divided into four semesters, focusing on the study of plant diseases, their diagnosis, and management. The MSc in Plant Pathology programme’s curriculum includes core subjects such as Mycology, Bacteriology, Virology, and advanced techniques in plant pathology. To be eligible for M.Sc

. Plant Pathology programme, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree in agriculture, horticulture, botany or a related field.  M.Sc. Plant Pathology programme’s graduates can pursue their careers in government agencies, private agricultural companies, research institutions, and academia, addressing critical challenges in sustainable agriculture.
