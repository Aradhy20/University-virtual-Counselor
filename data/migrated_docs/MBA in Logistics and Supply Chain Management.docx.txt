MBA in Logistics and Supply Chain Management

Tell me about MBA in Logistics and Supply Chain Management

MBA in Logistics and Supply Chain Management is a two-year programme that prepares students to manage the journey of products from the manufacturer to the customer. From planning to purchasing, storage, and transportation—this programme covers everything needed to run smooth business operations.At TMU, the programme is designed to meet today's industry needs

. Students learn through books, real-world case studies, field visits, projects, and internships. This way, they don’t just understand the theory but also learn how to solve real problems in the logistics world.This MBA is perfect for students who want to build a career in supply chain management, shipping, retail, manufacturing, or international trade

. It also provides access to a wide range of job roles and global opportunities.
