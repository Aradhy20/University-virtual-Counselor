 Q3: Are rooms in the TMU guest house air-conditioned?
 Q4: Guest house ki location aur environment kaisa hai?
 Q5: Who can book a room in the TMU guest house?
 Q6: TMU guest house me per night ka charge kitna hai?

A:

TMU’s guest house features 20+ air-conditioned, well-furnished rooms designed for maximum comfort. Facilities include complimentary Wi-Fi, TV, and clean surroundings maintained to high standards. The lush green surroundings provide a peaceful and serene environment on campus. Whether visiting for academic work, official business, or leisure, guests are offered a comfortable and convenient stay

. The stay charges are ₹1000 per night.

For more info, visit: https://www.tmu.ac.in/tmu/guest-house
 Note: Booking is subject to availability. Kindly connect with the university’s administration for prior arrangements.
 To reserve your stay, please contact the TMU guest house coordinator in advance.

Who Can Stay at the TMU Guest House?

Q1: Who is allowed to stay in the TMU guest house?
 Q2: Kya students ke parents ya guardians guest house me stay kar sakte hain?
 Q3: Are external examiners and faculty members allowed to stay at TMU guest house?
 Q4: TMU guest house kin logon ke liye available hota hai?
 Q5: Can I book the TMU guest house for my parents during a university event?

Q6: Kya guest house university visitors ke liye hi hai ya sab ke liye?
 Q7: What categories of guests are eligible to stay at the TMU campus guest house?

A:
 The TMU Guest House is available for a variety of guests including students' parents, guardians, visiting faculty, university guests, examiners, academic collaborators, and official delegates. It is ideal for both academic and personal visits, providing a safe and welcoming environment within the university campus.

For more info, visit: https://www.tmu.ac.in/tmu/guest-house
 Note: Stay charges are ₹1000 per night. Booking is subject to availability and must be done in advance.
 To reserve a room, please contact the TMU guest house coordinator through the university administration.
