. The programme curriculum covers core subjects, electives, and a focus on practical research, students can expect a well-rounded education. To be eligible for BCA (Hons) with Reserach programme

A: To be eligible for the BCA (Hons) with Research programme at TMU, candidates must have completed their 10+2 grade education in the science stream with a strong focus on Mathematics from a recognised institution

. A minimum aggregate score is usually required, although this may vary depending on the university’s regulations.Academic qualifications: Completion of 10+2 education with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as mandatory subjects, with a minimum required percentage as specified by the university.Prerequisite subjects: Having a strong background in Physics, Chemistry

, Chemistry, and Mathematics.Entrance Examination: At TMU, Candidates may be required to appear for an entrance examination to assess their readiness for the programme.
