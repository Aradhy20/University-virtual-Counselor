Q: Programme Details

A: The programme is designed to equip students with the knowledge and practical skills necessary for a successful career in radiological imaging. The curriculum covers a range of topics, including anatomy, radiographic positioning, and image analysis. With a duration of 2 years, this programme ensures a well-rounded education that meets industry standards.

Q: Full-Form Of MSc In RIT

A: Understanding the full form of MSc is essential for prospective students. MSc stands for Master of Science in Radiological Imaging Techniques, showcasing the programme's focus on scientific principles and advanced knowledge in radiological imaging techniques
