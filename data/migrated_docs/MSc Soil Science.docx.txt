MSc Soil Science

Tell me about MSc Soil Science

MSc in Soil Science at Teerthanker Mahaveer University is a two-year postgraduate programme, divided into four semesters, focused on the study of soil ecosystems, their properties, and management practices. The curriculum includes core subjects like soil chemistry, biology, and physics, along with electives such as environmental soil science and soil conservation. M.Sc

. Soil Science programme’s eligibility requires a Bachelor's degree in Agriculture, Horticulture, or a related field with a minimum aggregate marks, which vary depending on the college’s admission process.  M.Sc. Soil Science programme’s graduates can pursue careers as agricultural scientists, soil conservationists, environmental consultants, or research scientists.
