Course Overview – MD Dermatology

Q1: What is MD in Dermatology?
 Q2: MD Dermatology kya hai?
 Q3: What does the course include?
 Q4: Course me kya-kya sikhaya jata hai?
 Q5: Is it a clinical course?
 Q6: Kya yeh clinical programme hai?

A:
 MD Dermatology, Venereology & Leprosy is a 3-year clinical PG programme focused on the diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, nail, and venereal diseases. It also includes minor surgical procedures and cosmetic dermatology.
