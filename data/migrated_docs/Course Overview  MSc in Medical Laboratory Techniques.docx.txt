Course Overview – MSc in Medical Laboratory Techniques

Q1: What is MSc in Medical Laboratory Techniques all about?
 Q2: MSc Medical Lab Techniques course me kya sikhaya jata hai?
 Q3: Kya yeh course lab testing aur diagnostics pe focused hai?

A:
 MSc in Medical Laboratory Techniques is a 2-year postgraduate programme that focuses on advanced clinical diagnostics, lab management, pathology, microbiology, hematology, and biochemistry. It prepares students for senior technical and supervisory roles in healthcare labs.
