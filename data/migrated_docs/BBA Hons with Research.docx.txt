Tell me about BBA (Hons) with Research

BBA (Hons) with Research, which stands for Bachelor of Business Administration (Honours) with Research, is a four-year undergraduate programme divided into eight semesters, and the programme focuses on combining business administration with a strong research focus

. The programme curriculum includes key subjects like Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, and Research Methodology. To be eligible for BBA (Hons) with Research programme, candidates must have completed 10+2 with a minimum percentage of marks as required. BBA (Hons) with Research programme’s graduates can pursue their careers in business analysis, marketing, finance
