About MDS in Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics

 1. Course Overview – MDS in Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics

Q1: What is MDS in Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics?
 Q2: MDS Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics kya hai?
 Q3: What topics are covered in this programme?
 Q4: Is course mein kya padhaya jata hai?
 Q5: Does this course involve braces and jaw correction?
 Q6: Kya yeh course braces aur jaw alignment pe focused hai?

A:
 MDS in Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics is a 3-year postgraduate programme that focuses on diagnosing and correcting dental and facial irregularities. It includes training in braces, aligners, orthognathic surgery planning, and growth modulation.
