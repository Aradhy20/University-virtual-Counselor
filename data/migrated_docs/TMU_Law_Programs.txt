Answer:

TMU ka College of Law & Legal Studies BCI (Bar Council of India) approved hai aur quality legal education provide karta hai.

PROGRAMS OFFERED:
1. BA LLB (Hons) — 5-year Integrated Program
   - Eligibility: 12th pass (any stream) with minimum 45% marks
   - Duration: 5 years (10 semesters)
   - Admission: CLAT score / TMU entrance / Merit-based

2. LLB — 3-year Program
   - Eligibility: Graduation in any discipline with minimum 45% marks
   - Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)
   - Admission: CLAT score / TMU entrance / Merit-based

3. LLM — Master of Law
   - Eligibility: LLB degree with minimum 50% marks
   - Duration: 1 year (2 semesters) or 2 years
   - Specializations: Constitutional Law, Corporate Law, Criminal Law

APPROVAL: Bar Council of India (BCI) approved

KEY FEATURES:
- Moot Court Room: Courtroom-style moot court for practice advocacy
- Legal Aid Clinic: Community service through legal awareness programs
- Library: Law journals, case studies, bare acts, digital law databases
- Internship: Mandatory internships with courts, law firms, NGOs
- Guest Lectures: Practicing lawyers, judges, and legal experts
- Debates & Seminars: Regular legal debates, paper presentations

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES AFTER LAW:
- Advocate / Lawyer (Court Practice)
- Corporate Lawyer (Company Law)
- Legal Advisor / Consultant
- Judge (after Judicial Services Exam)
- Legal Officer in Government/Private organizations
- Public Prosecutor
- Legal Analyst
- Human Rights Lawyer
