MTech in Electrical Power and Energy System

Tell me about MTech in Electrical Power and Energy System

MTech Electrical Power and Energy System is a two-year postgraduate programme, divided into four semesters, that offers an in-depth study of power generation, transmission, distribution, and renewable energy systems. The curriculum includes core subjects like Power System Analysis, Electrical Machines, Energy Management

, Electrical Machines, Energy Management, and advanced topics such as Smart Grid Technology and Renewable Energy Systems. Eligibility requires a BTech in Electrical Engineering or a related field

. Graduates can pursue careers as Power Systems Engineers, Energy Consultants, and Research Scientists in leading energy companies and research institutions etc.
