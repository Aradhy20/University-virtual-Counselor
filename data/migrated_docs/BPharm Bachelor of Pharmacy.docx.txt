Tell me about BPharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy)

BPharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy) is a four-year undergraduate degree focused on pharmaceutical sciences, including drug formulation, chemistry, pharmacology, and healthcare. It prepares students for careers in the pharmaceutical industry, research, clinical practice, and regulatory affairs. In a post-pandemic nation, the need for pharmacy workers has grown manifold

. If you wish to pursue a Bachelor of Pharmacy, look no further as Teerthanker Mahaveer University(TMU) is your destination. In this guide, we will explore the programme details, admission process, career opportunities, and more.
