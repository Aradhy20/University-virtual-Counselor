Course Overview – Bachelor of Optometry

Q1: What is Bachelor of Optometry course about?
 Q2: Bachelor of Optometry mein kya sikhaya jaata hai?
 Q3: Can you explain the BOptom programme at TMU?

A:
 The Bachelor of Optometry (B.Optom) is a 4-year undergraduate programme focused on vision care, clinical diagnosis, and optical technology. It trains students in the science of examining, diagnosing, and managing visual disorders.

 The curriculum covers anatomy, ocular physiology, clinical examination of the eye, refraction, contact lenses, dispensing optics, and optometric instrumentation.
