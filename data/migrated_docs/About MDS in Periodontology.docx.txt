About MDS in Periodontology

1. Course Overview – MDS in Periodontology

Q1: What is MDS in Periodontology?
 Q2: MDS Periodontology kya hai?
 Q3: What will I study in this course?
 Q4: MDS Periodontology mein kya padhaya jata hai?
 Q5: What does Periodontology focus on?
 Q6: Periodontology kis cheez se related hai?

A:
 MDS in Periodontology is a 3-year postgraduate dental programme that focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of periodontal (gum) diseases. It also covers surgical procedures related to oral implants.
