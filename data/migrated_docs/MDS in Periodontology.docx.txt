MDS in Periodontology

Tell me about MDS in Periodontology

MDS in Periodontology at Teerthanker Mahaveer University (TMU), Moradabad, paves the way for a successful career in dentistry by offering advanced clinical training and in-depth knowledge in the field, setting a benchmark for dental education in India

. With NAAC ‘A’ Garde accreditation and recognition, it also stands among the top private dental colleges in India, with a strong focus on clinical training, ensuring that students gain hands-on experience in diagnosing and treating periodontal diseases, including surgical and non-surgical interventions.
