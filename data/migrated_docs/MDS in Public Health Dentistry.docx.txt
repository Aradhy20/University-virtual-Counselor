Tell me about MDS in Public Health Dentistry

MDS in Public Health Dentistry is a strategic choice for those aiming to make a meaningful impact on community oral health, focusing on preventive care, health promotion, and population-based dental solutions in the ever-evolving field of dentistry

. Teerthanker Mahaveer University (TMU) in Moradabad proudly presents its MDS In Public Health Dentistry programme, designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for a successful and fulfilling career. Here with excellence and commitment, TMU stands among the top private dental colleges in India.
