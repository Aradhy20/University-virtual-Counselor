Frequently Asked Questions about BTech (AICTE Approved) in Computer Science & Engineering

Q: What is the Full Form of BTech CSE?

A: The full form of BTech CSE is Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering. It is a four-year undergraduate degree programme that focuses on the theoretical foundations and practical applications of computing, programming, software development, algorithms, data structures, computer networks, databases, and emerging technologies like AI, machine learning, and cybersecurity.

Q: BTech CSE Eligibility Criteria.

A: To be eligible for admission to the BTech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) programme in India, candidates must generally meet the following criteria:Qualification: 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects

, along with Chemistry/Computer Science/IT or equivalent.Minimum Marks: 45%–50% aggregate generally (5% relaxation for reserved categories).Entrance Exam: JEE Main/state-level/university-level exam (as applicable).Age: Minimum 17 years; no upper age limit in most cases.Lateral Entry: Diploma holders in engineering may apply for direct admission to 2nd year.
