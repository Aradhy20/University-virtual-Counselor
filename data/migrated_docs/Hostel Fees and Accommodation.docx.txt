Hostel Fees and Accommodation

4 Seater (Common Bath) With Cooler

Q1: What is the hostel fee for a 4-seater room with common bath and cooler (non-Jain)?
 Q2: Jain student ke liye 4-seater common bathroom cooler room ki fee kitni hai?
 Q3: Kya ₹60,000 me mess charge bhi included hai 4-seater common bath room ke liye?
 Q4: 4-seater common bathroom hostel Jain aur non-Jain dono ke liye kitna padta hai?

Q5: Jain students ko kitna discount milta hai 4-seater common bath room pe?
 Q6: Mess charges alag se kitne hain 4-seater common bath with cooler ke liye?
 Q7: Jain ke lodging aur mess breakup kya hai 4-seater common bathroom category me?

Answer:
 For the 4-seater (common bath) with cooler hostel room, the total fee for non-Jain students is ₹60,000 per year, which includes ₹32,000 for lodging and ₹28,000 for mess. For Jain students, the total fee is ₹50,400 per year, including ₹22,400 lodging and ₹28,000 mess. The mess charge is fixed at ₹28,000 per year across all categories and is included in the total hostel fee.

For more info, visit: https://www.tmu.ac.in/tmu/hostel
 Note: Jain students receive a 30% fee exemption on common bathroom categories and a 15% exemption on attached bathroom categories (lodging only).
 To book your hostel, please call your counsellor. Full payment is required at the time of booking.
