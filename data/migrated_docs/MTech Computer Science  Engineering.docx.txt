Tell me about MTech (Computer Science & Engineering)

MTech Computer Science & Engineering is a two-year postgraduate programme designed to deepen knowledge and expertise in advanced computing technologies

. The programme spans four semesters, covering a comprehensive syllabus that includes core subjects like algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science. Eligibility requires a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field

, Information Technology, or a related field, and a qualifying entrance exam score may also require. MTech Computer Science and Engineering graduates are well-equipped for careers in software development, data analytics, cybersecurity, and research, with opportunities in both industry and academia.
