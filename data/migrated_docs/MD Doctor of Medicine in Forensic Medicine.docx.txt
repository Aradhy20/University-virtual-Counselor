MD (Doctor of Medicine) in Forensic Medicine

Tell me about MD (Doctor of Medicine) in Forensic Medicine

MD (Doctor of Medicine) in Forensic Medicine is a Non Clinical Speciality course. Doctor of Medicine in Forensic science is a three-year postgraduation course. Forensic science focuses on application of the scientific principles for crime investigation

. Subjects studied under this degree are Forensic Science, Forensic Toxicology, Medical Jurisprudence and legal procedure, Law & Medicine and Autopsy. The curriculum provides basic and specialized services in relation with medico-legal responsibilities in practice of medicine and process of crime investigation.
