
# COMMON ADMISSION FAQS (HINGLISH)

Q: Placement kaisa hai? Job lag jayegi pakka?
A: Dekhiye, TMU ka placement record shandaar hai. Highest package 60 LPA raha hai. Average package 5-7 LPA rehta hai. 100% placement assistance milti hai. Agar student mehnat kare, toh job pakka lagti hai. Top recruiters mein TCS, Wipro, Infosys included hain.

Q: Hostel ka khana kaisa hai?
A: Hostel mein sirf Pure Veg khana milta hai, jo ghar jaisa hygienic hota hai. Menu weekly change hota hai - Dal makhani, Paneer, Roti, Rice sab milta hai. Mess safai ka poora dhyan rakhti hai.

Q: Girls ke liye safety kaisi hai?
A: Safety hamari top priority hai. Campus poori tarah secure hai - 24x7 guards aur CCTV cameras lage hain. Girls hostel campus ke andar hi hai aur warden ki strict monitoring rehti hai. Entry-exit timings fix hain. Parents can rest assured.

Q: Kya direct admission ho jayega bina exam ke?
A: Kuch courses mein merit-based (12th marks) direct admission hota hai. Lekin technical courses (jaise B.Tech) ke liye JEE ya TMU ka entrance test dena pad sakta hai. Aap apne marks bataiye, main check kar lungi.

Q: Koi scholarship ya discount milega?
A: Haan ji, bilkul!
1. Jain Minority students ke liye 50% tuition fee waiver hai.
2. Merit Scholarship: 12th mein >90% marks par scholarship milti hai.
3. Sports quota bhi available hai.
Aapka koi category lagta hai kya?

Q: Ragging hoti hai kya campus mein?
A: Bilkul nahi! TMU ek 100% Ragging-Free campus hai. Strict laws hain aur Anti-Ragging committee active rehti hai. Senior students bahut supportive hain 'Didi/Bhaiya' wala culture hai.

Q: Mobile phone allowed hai hostel mein?
A: Haan ji, mobile phones allowed hain. Bas class timings mein silent rakhna hota hai. Pure campus mein Wi-Fi connectivity bhi available hai.

Q: Weekend pe bahar ja sakte hain?
A: Sunday ko outing allowed hoti hai lekin specific timings ke sath. First year students ke liye gate pass system hota hai jisme parents ki permission lag sakti hai. Safety ke liye yeh zaroori hai.

Q: Uniform pehenni padegi kya?
A: Haan, professional environment maintain karne ke liye uniform compulsory hai. Isse discipline aur equality bani rehti hai.

Q: Moradabad kaise pahunch sakte hain campus?
A: TMU campus Delhi Road (NH-24) par hai. Moradabad Railway station se bas 10-15 km door hai. Auto aur cabs easily mil jate hain. Delhi se drive karke 3-3.5 ghante lagte hain.

Q: Syllabus tough toh nahi hai? Hindi medium se hoon.
A: Chinta mat kijiye! Teachers bahut supportive hain aur Hindi-English mix mein samjhate hain. Initially bridge classes bhi hoti hain taaki Hindi medium students ko dikkat na aaye.
