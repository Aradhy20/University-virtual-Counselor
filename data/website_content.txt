Teerthanker Mahaveer University (TMU) Overview
TMU is a top private university in Moradabad, UP, India.
It offers programmes in:
- Agriculture
- Computer Science
- Dental
- Education
- Engineering
- Fine Arts
- Law
- Management
- Medical
- Nursing
- Paramedical
- Pharmacy
- Physical Education
- Physiotherapy

Highlights:
- 85% Outstanding Placement Rate
- Highest Package Offered: 60 LPA
- National and International Collaborations
- NIRF Innovation Category recognition
- NAAC 'A' Grade accredited
- 12B Status from UGC
