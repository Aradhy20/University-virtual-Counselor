=== UNIVERSITY OVERVIEW ===
Teerthanker Mahaveer University (TMU) is a top private university located at N.H.-9, Delhi Road, Moradabad - 244001, Uttar Pradesh, India.
TMU stands for Teerthanker Mahaveer University.
TMU has a lush green campus spanning over 140 acres in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh.
TMU was established under the aegis of the Digamber Jain community.
TMU has 14 on-campus colleges and independent departments.
TMU offers over 150 programmes at diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels.
TMU has over 28,000 alumni worldwide.
TMU employs 1,000+ dedicated faculty and staff members.
Approximately 3 hours from Delhi by road. Well connected by rail and road.
