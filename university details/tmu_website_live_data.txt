Q: What is TMU's placement rate?
A: TMU has an outstanding placement rate of 85% (as of 2024-25). The highest package ever offered to a TMU student is 60 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum).

Q: How many programmes does TMU offer?
A: TMU offers over 150 programmes across 14 on-campus colleges and independent departments, catering to a wide range of academic interests.

Q: What industry collaborations does TMU have?
A: TMU has established strong ties with leading industries including IBM, AutoCAD, TCS-iON, Apple, NVidia, BOSCH, and Lenskart. These enhance practical exposure and industry readiness for students.

Q: What is TMU's campus size?
A: TMU's lush green campus spans over 130 acres, equipped with state-of-the-art facilities including sports complexes, modern libraries, and cutting-edge laboratories.

Q: How many alumni does TMU have?
A: TMU has a notable alumni network of over 28,000 successful individuals contributing to various fields globally.

Q: What is TMU's NIRF ranking?
A: TMU secured a position in the 51-100 rank band in the NIRF Innovation Category, reflecting its commitment to fostering innovation in education.

Q: Is TMU following NEP 2020?
A: Yes. TMU is proactively implementing the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, adopting a national credit framework, redesigning the curriculum, and focusing on Outcome-Based Education (OBE).
