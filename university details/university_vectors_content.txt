C: General Q: What is the objective behind TMU's scholarship programs? A: At TMU, scholarships aim to reward meritorious and deserving students to ensure that financial constraints do not hinder their pursuit of higher education.

C: General Q: How many scholarships or concessions can a student avail in total? A: A student can avail the Academic Scholarship plus a maximum of three additional scholarships/concessions.

C: General Q: Is there a fee concession for paying the course fee annually? A: Yes, candidates who pay the full annual fee at once receive an additional 5% concession on net tuition fees. This is not available for half-yearly payments.

C: Jain Studies Scholarship Q: Does TMU offer scholarships for Jain Studies? A: Yes, TMU offers special scholarships for students enrolled in various programs if they pursue Jain Studies.

C: Jain Studies Scholarship Q: What is the fee waiver provided under the Jain Studies scholarship? A: Eligible students can get 50% tuition fee waiver for specific programs, 20% for others, and hostel lodging fee waivers (30% common, 15% attached bathroom).

C: Academic Scholarships Q: Are there scholarships based on marks in qualifying exams? A: Yes. Students securing 60–69.9% get 10%, 70–79.9% get 15%, 80–89.9% get 25%, and 90% & above get 50%.

C: Academic Scholarships Q: Is the entry-level scholarship applicable to all programs? A: No, it excludes D.El.Ed. (BTC), collaborative programs, Dental, Medical, Allied Programs, and Agriculture.

C: Academic Scholarships Q: What scholarships are available for B.Tech CSE (IBM collaboration)? A: Students securing 70–79.99% in 10+2 get 10%, 80–89.99% get 20%, 90% & above get 25%.

C: Academic Scholarships Q: Are there scholarships based on CUET scores? A: Yes. CUET scores offer: 60–69.99% – 10%, 70–79.99% – 15%, 80–89.99% – 25%, 90% & above – 50%.

C: Competitive Exams Q: What scholarships are available based on JEE (Main) and UP-CET ranks for B.Tech? A: JEE (Main) ranks up to 15,000: 90%, 15,001–25,000: 50%, 25,001–50,000: 20%; similar for UP-CET.

C: Competitive Exams Q: What scholarships are available for MBA/MCA aspirants with CAT/MAT/CMAT scores? A: 90–95 percentile: 20%, 96–97 percentile: 40%, 98 & above: 100%.

C: Alumni Scholarships Q: Are there scholarships for TMU alumni or Madan Swarup Inter College pass-outs? A: Yes. 60–69.9%: 15%, 70–79.9%: 20%, 80–89.9%: 30%, 90% & above: 50%.

C: Chancellor & Sibling Q: Do wards of TMU staff receive any scholarships? A: Yes. Chancellor’s Scholarship provides 20% tuition fee waiver per year for the entire program.

C: Chancellor & Sibling Q: Is there a sibling scholarship available? A: Yes. A 10% tuition fee discount is provided to the sibling with the lower tuition fee, applicable only in the first year.

C: Sports Scholarships Q: Are there scholarships for national or international level sportspersons? A: Yes. Olympic/World Level Gold: 100%, Silver/Bronze: 80%, Inter-varsity Zonal Medalists: 60%, Recognized national level participation: 15%.

C: Sports Scholarships Q: Are there scholarships for district or state-level players? A: Yes. Cluster/State participation: 10%, District or trial-based players: 5%.
