=== MEDICAL COLLEGE & RESEARCH CENTRE ===

Q: What are the MBBS details at TMU?
A: MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery) at TMU is a 5.5-year programme (4.5 years + 1 year compulsory internship). It is approved by NMC (National Medical Commission). Admission is based on NEET UG Merit. TMU's Medical College is ranked No.1 in UP by IIRF Rankings 2025. Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 50% marks in PCB and pass marks in English.

Q: What is the fee for MBBS at TMU?
A: MBBS Tuition Fee: Rs. 7,20,000/- per year. University Examination Fee: Rs. 16,000/- per year. Enrollment Fee (one time): Rs. 1,000/-. Admission Processing Fee (one time): Rs. 2,500/-.

Q: What are MD programmes at TMU and their fees?
A: MD (Doctor of Medicine) programmes at TMU are 3-year postgraduate programmes. Admission is based on NEET PG Merit. Specialisations: Anatomy, Anaesthesiology, Biochemistry, Community Medicine, Dermatology/Venereology/Leprosy, Forensic Medicine, General Medicine, Microbiology, Paediatrics, Pathology, Pharmacology, Physiology, Psychiatry, Radio-Diagnosis/Radiology, Respiratory Medicine/TB & Chest. Tuition Fee: Rs. 54,000/- per semester.

Q: What are MS programmes at TMU?
A: MS (Master of Surgery) programmes at TMU are 3-year postgraduate programmes. Specialisations: ENT/Oto-Rhinolaryngology, General Surgery, Ophthalmology, Orthopaedics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology. Admission is based on NEET PG Merit.

Q: What is MSc Medical at TMU?
A: MSc Medical programmes at TMU are 2-year programmes. Fee: Rs. 45,000/- per semester. Specialisations: Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Pharmacology. Eligibility: MBBS/BDS/B.Sc. Nursing/relevant science degree with minimum 50% marks.

=== DENTAL COLLEGE & RESEARCH CENTRE ===

Q: What is the BDS programme at TMU and its fees?
A: BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) at TMU is a 4+1 year programme (4 years + 1 year compulsory internship). Tuition Fee: Rs. 6,30,000/- per year. University Examination Fee: Rs. 90,000/- (for whole programme - Rs. 25,000 in 1st year, Rs. 65,000 in 3rd year). Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 50% marks in PCB. Admission: NEET UG Merit. TMU Dental College is ranked 4th in UP by IIRF Rankings 2025.

Q: What MDS programmes does TMU offer?
A: MDS (Master of Dental Surgery) programmes at TMU are 3-year postgraduate courses. Admission is based on NEET PG Merit. Fee: Rs. 3,60,000/- per year. Specialisations available: Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics, Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery, Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics, Prosthodontics & Crown & Bridge, Paedodontics & Preventive Dentistry, Periodontology, Public Health Dentistry, Oral Medicine & Radiology, Oral Pathology & Microbiology.

=== SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME ===

Q: What scholarships does TMU offer for 2026-27?
A: TMU offers a comprehensive scholarship scheme:
1. Entry Level Scholarship (UG/PG/Diploma/Collaborative programmes, excluding Medical/Dental/D.El.Ed.):
   - 60-69.9% in qualifying exam: 10% scholarship on tuition fee
   - 70-79.9%: 15% scholarship
   - 80-89.9%: 25% scholarship
   - 90% and above: 50% scholarship
   (Available in admission year only. Not applicable to 1-year or less programmes.)
2. Jain Student Scholarship: Up to 50% exemption in Tuition Fees and up to 30% in Hostel Fees for the entire duration of the programme (not applicable for D.El.Ed./BTC, MDS, Medical).
3. Chancellor's Scholarship for Staff Wards
4. Special Scholarship for Meritorious Sports Aspirants (District/State/National/International level)
5. Scholarship for Wards of Visually Impaired Employees of TMU
6. Multiple scholarships cannot be combined/stacked.
